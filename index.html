U.S. Women's National Soccer Team

U.S. Women's National Team Info

The United States Women's national soccer team (USWNT) is governed by the United States Soccer Federation. This team is the most successful in the international women's soccer. They've won 3 Women's World Cup titles, 4 Olympic women's gold medals, 7 CONCACAF (the Confederation of North, Central American and Caribbean Association Football) Gold Cup wins, and 10 Algarve Cups.

The team is made up 4 goalkeepers: Jane Campbell, Adrianna Franch, Ashlyn Harris, and Alyssa Naeher, 8 defenders: Abby Dahlkemper, Sofia Huerta, Kelly O'Hara, Becky Sauerbrunn, Casey Short, Taylor Smith, Emily Sonnett, and Chioma Ubogagu, 6 midfielders: Juile Ertz, Lindsey Horan, Carli Lloyd, Allie Long, Samantha Mewis, and Andi Sullivan, and 4 forwards: Alex Morgan, Christen Press, Megan Rapinoe,and Lynn Williams.
